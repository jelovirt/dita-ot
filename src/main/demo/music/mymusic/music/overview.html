Music library, converted from iTunes XML to DITA

The frame on the left is generated in the browser based on a DITA map, pointing to information encoded in the DITA music specialization (see the DITA Open Toolkit download page). The links are all to DITA XML files which will resolve in the browser.

Note that the display is not very well NLS enabled, but that may be updated for at least a couple of languages in the future.

Much of this music can be purchased at your local independent music shop.
